## What is Bread?

Bread solves the fundamental challenge of converting cryptocurrency into local fiat currencies. Whether you're building a fintech app, a crypto exchange, or any application that needs to bridge the gap between digital assets and traditional banking, Bread provides the infrastructure you need.
